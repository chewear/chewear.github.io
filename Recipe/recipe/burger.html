Burger and Fries Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 medium russet potatoes
  • 8 cups peanut oil for frying
  • 14 ounces ground chuck
  • 4 thick slices American cheese
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce (such as Frank's Red Hot)
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 2 medium sesame seed burger buns
  • ½ cup shredded iceberg lettuce
  • 2 thick slices tomato
  • 2 tablespoons diced onion
  • 1 tablespoon pickle slices
  • 1 teaspoon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes in ½-inch wide strips.
  2. Place the potato strips in a large bowl filled with warm water and soak for 20 minutes.
  3. Pour oil in a Dutch oven and heat over medium-high heat until it reaches 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  4. Line a rimmed baking sheet with paper towels and set a wire rack on top.
  5. Drain the soaked potatoes well and dry them thoroughly with paper towels.
  6. Add ½ of the dried potatoes to the hot oil and cook, stirring often, until they soften slightly, about 3 minutes. Remove potatoes to the wire rack to drain.
  7. Repeat the previous step with the remaining potatoes.
  8. Maintain oil temperature at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for finishing fries just before serving.
  9. Divide ground chuck into 4 portions and flatten to form ¼-inch thick patties.
  10. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add burger patties to the hot skillet and cook undisturbed, until a brown crust has formed around the edges, 2 to 3 minutes.
  11. Flip the patties, press down with a spatula to flatten, and cook for 2 more minutes.
  12. Flip the patties again, press down once more, then top each patty with a slice of cheese; cook for 1 minute.
  13. While the burgers cook, spread mustard and ketchup on the bottom halves of the buns.
  14. Drizzle hot sauce on the top halves of the buns, then spread mayonnaise over the top halves.
  15. Layer the bottom halves of the buns with lettuce, tomato slices, pickles, and onions.
  16. Invert 1 patty onto a second patty so the cheese sides are touching. Repeat with remaining 2 patties.
  17. Place a stacked patty onto each top bun, and cover with a bottom bun, condiment-side down. Wrap burgers in foil.
  18. Return the fries to the hot oil and cook until golden brown, 3 to 4 minutes.
  19. Drain the fries on paper towels and sprinkle with salt.
  20. Serve the burgers immediately with the fries.
