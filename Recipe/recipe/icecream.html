Ice Cream Sandwich Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 24 vanilla ice cream sandwiches, unwrapped
  • 2 (8 ounce) containers whipped topping (such as Cool Whip®), thawed
  • 1 (12 ounce) jar hot fudge ice cream topping, warmed
  • 1 (12 ounce) jar caramel ice cream topping
  • ¼ cup chopped pecans, or to taste

Steps

  1. Arrange a layer of ice cream sandwiches in the bottom of a 9x13-inch dish
  2. top with a layer of whipped topping, hot fudge topping, and caramel topping
  3. Repeat layering with remaining ice cream sandwiches, whipped topping, hot fudge topping, and caramel topping, ending with a top layer of whipped topping.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Sprinkle with pecans.
  6. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and freeze until set, about 30 minutes.
